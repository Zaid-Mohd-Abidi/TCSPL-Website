Career Progression – We are looking at providing careers and not merely jobs at MSPL. We ensure that the needs of the organisation and those of individual employees are matched at all times. We provide ample opportunity for growth to those who show their dedication, commitment and deliverables.

 Training & Development – For our employees on board there's always an opportunity to develop one's own skills, whether by means of continuous grooming in soft skills or process based training programs tailored to suit a particular role or brand.Some of our staff members (product specific) get trained at relevant international locations.

 Equal Opportunities Policy - The company is committed to providing equal opportunities to all staff of and applicants to the Company and does not discriminate, directly or indirectly on the basis of sex, sexual orientation (whether perceived or actual), marital status, race (to include color, nationality,ethnic or national origins), religion or disability.
